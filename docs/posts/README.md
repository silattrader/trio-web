# Launch posts — pure copy-paste

Each file in this directory is a single platform's post, ready to paste
without editing. Top-of-file HTML comments tell you where to submit and
when.

| File | Platform | When |
|---|---|---|
| `hackernews.md` | Hacker News (Show HN) | Mon-Wed 7-9 AM Pacific |
| `twitter-thread.md` | X / Twitter (6-tweet thread) | ~9 AM ET weekday |
| `linkedin.md` | LinkedIn (long-form post) | Tue-Thu morning local |
| `reddit-algotrading.md` | r/algotrading (discussion) | After HN traffic settles |
| `cold-email.md` | B2B prospect outreach | Personalize per recipient |

## Recommended cadence

If you launch the whole sequence in one week:

| Day | Platform | Why this order |
|---|---|---|
| Mon 9 AM ET | Hacker News | Permissive audience for technical OSS — biggest signal generator |
| Tue 9 AM ET | X thread | Ride HN momentum; crossposts amplify |
| Wed AM | LinkedIn | Different audience — recruiters / fund ops |
| Thu PM | r/algotrading | After you've handled the first wave + have answers ready |
| Fri+ | Cold email | Use engagement signals from public posts to warm the pitch |

**Don't do all on the same day.** Each platform's algorithm notices when content is being broadcast vs organically posted.

## Success metrics by platform

| Platform | Reasonable bar |
|---|---|
| Hacker News | Front page (top 30) for 4+ hrs · 50+ comments · 100+ stars |
| X | 1000+ thread impressions · 5+ replies from quant/fintech accounts |
| LinkedIn | 5000+ impressions · 3+ DMs from fund/family-office contacts |
| r/algotrading | 50+ upvotes · 20+ technical-substance comments |
| Cold email | 1 reply per 10 sends · 1 booked call per 30 sends |

If a platform underperforms its bar, **don't double down there** — it's a signal the audience isn't there. Move on to the next.
